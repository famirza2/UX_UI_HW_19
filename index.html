MY UX SKILLSET

USER RESEARCH

I prioritize understanding user needs through primary research methods such as conducting user interviews, surveys, and usability testing. I also utilize secondary research methods like heuristic evaluation, site mapping, and competitive analysis.

During research, I use a variety of tools to ensure effective communication and organization, including Figma, FigJam, Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, and Zoom.

USER EMPATHY

To understand users comprehensively, I collect and synthesize research data into deliverables such as affinity maps, personas, and empathy maps. These help me identify pain points and situations that require solutions by depicting what users think, feel, and value.

Such insights are used to design user flows and journeys illustrating how users behave and feel when interacting with solutions, as well as how they might be discovered. By prioritizing users throughout the design process, I produce solutions that sincerely address their needs and expectations.

QUALITATIVE INTERVIEWS

I craft research plans with specific objectives and open-ended questions to generate detailed participant responses.

I primarily document conversations via audio recording and actively listen during interviews, so that I may ask probing questions and gain deeper insights.

When usability tests accompany interviews, I also create 3-5 tasks for completion and document their success rates. Video and screen recordings may also take place to document and closely evaluate what users experience.
