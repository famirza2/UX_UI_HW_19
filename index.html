ABOUT ME

UX/UI designer with a love for storytelling. Focused on designing intuitive, efficient, and delightful interfaces that prioritize user needs and tell compelling stories.

Proficiencies include UI Design and UX Research, using tools such as Figma, FigJam, InVision, and Adobe XD.

MY UX SKILLSET

USER RESEARCH

To understand user needs, I conduct primary user research using qualitative and quantitative methods such as interviews, surveys, and usability testing.

I also employ secondary research methods such as heuristic evaluation, site mapping, and competitive analysis to assess user behaviors and preferences.

USER EMPATHY

I collect and synthesize research data into deliverables such as affinity maps, personas, and empathy maps to gain insights into scenarios that require solutions.

I utilize such deliverables to design flows and journeys that prioritize the user's perspective, resulting in solutions that address their needs and expectations.

USER INTERFACE DESIGN

I produce wireframes and prototypes to mock up intuitive interfaces aligned with user needs and business goals, and refine designs through iterative testing.

I create and manage pixel-perfect components and UI patterns for scalability, take a mobile-first approach to responsive optimization, and enhance experiences with micro-interactions and animation.
